Energy Patterns for Mobile Apps

We are a group of researchers from INESC-ID creating an open catalogue of energy-related patterns in mobile applications. We'd love to count on you to make this a thorough catalogue and available to the mobile development community. Our goal is to share the knowledge across all developers and make mobile apps more energy efficient.

Lead Researchers: Luis Cruz, Rui Abreu.
