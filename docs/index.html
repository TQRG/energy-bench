Energy Patterns for Mobile Apps

This is an open catalogue of energy-related patterns in mobile applications. Our goal is to share the knowledge across all developers and make mobile apps more energy efficient. We'd love to count on you to make this a thorough catalogue and available to the mobile development community. Help us spread the word. Tweet
