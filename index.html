Energy Issues Dataset for Mobile Apps

We are a group of researchers from INESC-ID creating an open database of energy-related issues in mobile applications. We'd love to count on you to make this a thorough dataset and available to the mobile development community. Our goal is to share the knowledge across all developers and make mobile apps more energy efficient. If you know any issue that was draining energy in one of your projects, please share it with the community by filling the form below.

Lead Researchers: Luis Cruz, Rui Abreu.
